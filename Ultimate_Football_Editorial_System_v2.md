# Ultimate Football Editorial System v2

## Goal

Build a football publication that earns through authority first,
affiliate marketing second.

---

# Editorial Philosophy

1.  Every article must solve a reader's intent.
2.  Never publish obvious AI output.
3.  Every article should connect to 3-8 other pages.
4.  Affiliate links are recommendations, never advertisements.
5.  Every article should be updateable.

---

# Site Architecture

## Pillars

- Match Reviews
- Match Previews
- Tactical Analysis
- Player Profiles
- Player Ratings
- Transfer News
- Football Gear Reviews
- Buying Guides
- Comparisons
- Evergreen Guides

Cluster each pillar with dozens of supporting articles.

---

# Universal Writing Rules

- Reading level: Grade 7-9
- Paragraphs: 1-3 sentences
- H2 every 250-350 words
- H3 where needed
- Use bullets before long paragraphs
- Use tables whenever comparing
- Active voice
- Avoid AI clichés and filler

Forbidden phrases: - In today's fast-paced... - Whether you're a
beginner... - It is worth mentioning... - Dive into... - Delve into... -
Game changer... - Revolutionize...

# Internal Linking

Every article links to: - 2 parent articles - 3 sibling articles - 2
child articles

Money pages should receive the most internal links.

# Image Rules
