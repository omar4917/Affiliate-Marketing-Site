Whether you are a beginner or looking to refine your technique, technical repetition is the key to mastery. These 5 essential drills focus on the foundational aspects of the game: first touch, spatial awareness, passing speed, and control under pressure.

---

## 1. The Wall Pass & Turn (Solo Drill)
* **Goal:** Improve first touch, receiving on the half-turn, and passing accuracy.
* **Setup:** Place a cone 5 yards away from a solid brick wall or rebounder.
* **Execution:**
  1. Pass the ball firmly against the wall.
  2. As the ball returns, receive it with the inside of your back foot to turn around the cone.
  3. Dribble around the cone, return to the start, and repeat with your other foot.

## 2. The Cone Slalom (Dribbling)
* **Goal:** Improve close control and agility using different parts of the foot.
* **Setup:** Place 8 to 10 cones in a straight line, spaced 1 yard apart.
* **Execution:**
  1. Dribble through the cones as fast as possible.
  2. Focus on using different foot surfaces: inside only, outside only, sole rolls, and both feet.
  3. Keep your head up as much as possible to simulate looking for teammates.

## 3. The Rondo (4v1 or 5v2)
* **Goal:** Develop rapid one-touch passing, positional adjustment, and defensive pressing.
* **Setup:** Set up a 10x10 yard square.
* **Execution:**
  1. Attackers occupy the edges, and one or two defenders stand in the center.
  2. Attackers try to keep possession by passing the ball.
  3. If a defender intercepts, the attacker who made the mistake enters the center.
